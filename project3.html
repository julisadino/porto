This is my final project where I'm developing a fuzzy logic control system to regulate CO‚ÇÇ levels in controlled environments. The idea came from the challenge of maintaining optimal growing conditions in agricultural settings, where traditional on-off control systems often cause fluctuations that can stress plants.

I chose fuzzy logic because it mimics human decision-making better than conventional controllers. Instead of just "turn on" or "turn off," the system can make gradual adjustments like "slightly increase ventilation" or "moderately reduce CO‚ÇÇ injection." This approach helps maintain more stable environmental conditions while reducing energy waste from constant switching.
