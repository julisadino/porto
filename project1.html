IoT Based Water Level Monitoring System

Year 2024
Platform ESP32 & Blynk

How I Made it (with team)

In my 4th semester, I took a course called Instrumentation and Control from my department. This course led us to learn more deeply than before about electrical and electronics. My final project with my team was an IoT-based water level monitoring system integrated in real time with Blynk. We developed a small scale prototype dam model and used an ESP32 as a microcontroller integrated with a water level sensor to collect real time data from the reservoir.

Technologies Used

ESP32 Arduino IDE C++ Blynk IoT Iduino S045 Sensor

System was designed to automatically control the dam gate using a servo motor. When the water level reached a predetermined threshold, the ESP32 would trigger the servo to open the prototype dam gate, allowing water to flow out and preventing overflow. The water level data was continuously monitored and displayed on the Blynk app.
